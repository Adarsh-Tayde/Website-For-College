About Us

VISSION

To groom Motivated, Self-esteemed, Creative and Oriented Mechanical Engineers to fulfill the demand of industries and society.

MISSION

To Develop Industry Oriented Manpower to accept the challenges of Globalization by,
M1: Imparting Mechanical Engineering knowledge through trained faculty in conducive environment,
M2: Creating awareness about the needs of Mechanical industries through alumni and Industry- Institute interactions
M3: Encouraging them to think innovatively and introduce them to various research activities
M4: Supporting them to groom in all aspects like communication, interpersonal skills and Professional skills.
